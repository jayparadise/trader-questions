NBA - Pulling & Reactivating Teams


1.  Make sure X notifications are switched on and keep a close eye on them. https://x.com/Underdognba is the best source.

2.  Player/Team deactivation decision tree


** IMPORTANT NOTE **

We should be pulling if something affects the team. Just because one comp pulls doesn’t mean we should. 365 often pull both teams down straight away even when injury news only affects one team. They then get the non-impacted team back up within a matter of a few minutes.


Protocol: if there’s no news on a team we should be keeping them live even if a single comp like 365 pull and then just closely monitor.

3.  Deactivating a team:

  • Method 1: Go to ORR >> filter by team >> uncheck all active players
  • Method 2: Go to comps screen, search by team and deactivate players. Helpful to sort by player name and just uncheck each player once.

Deactivating players via for the ORR

5.  As soon as a team has been deactivated, the job at hand is to keep a close eye on relevant comps to see when they put the team(s) back up (bet365, Draftkings and Fanduel should be considered our main comps to monitor)

6. As soon as comps release, we need to work with urgency and get our players live again ASAP.

You may need to turn AT on for everyone to update lines. Or shut AT off for everyone and manually update all the affected lines.

If, for example, Fanduel have players up but bet365 don’t, then this is good enough for us to get players up as well. In other words, don’t wait on all comps as our reference.

Reactivating Players

Via COMPS SCREEN

  1. Make sure lines are updated from comps. Sometimes just one comp has updated and others still have old lines/prices.
  2. You may need to shut off AT for everyone and manually update all changed lines/prices.

  1. Filter table by team and sort by player name (this gets all stats together for each player)

  1. Toggle auto-lines ON for each player and each stat (i.e. each row)
  1. This will make the player active for ALL stats but will only activate O/U for the specific stat toggle that was switched on

  1. Double check O/U lines were properly activated and updated.
